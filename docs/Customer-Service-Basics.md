# Customer Service Basics

Customer service is the foundation of every successful organization. Every interaction should leave the customer feeling respected, understood, and supported.

## Core Principles

- Active Listening
- Professional Communication
- Empathy
- Problem Solving
- Documentation
- Policy Compliance
- Confidentiality
- Accountability

## Best Practices

- Greet customers professionally.
- Verify customer information.
- Listen without interrupting.
- Ask clarifying questions.
- Provide accurate information.
- Confirm the resolution.
- Thank the customer.
- Document every interaction accurately.

## Goals

- Customer Satisfaction
- First Contact Resolution
- Professionalism
- Accuracy
- Efficiency
